House building

Every family deserves a safe place to call home. At Africare, we construct durable, affordable houses for vulnerable communities across Africa, replacing unstable shelters with secure, weather-resistant homes. Our housing projects prioritize families affected by poverty, natural disasters, or displacement, ensuring children grow up in environments that protect and nurture them. Strong homes build strong futures. Support our mission to provide safe shelter and transform lives. We work alongside local builders using sustainable materials and techniques, empowering communities through training and fair-wage labor. Each home includes proper sanitation and clean water access, dramatically improving health outcomes.
